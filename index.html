Akan names

THIS WHY GHANA IS UNQUE WITH THEIR AKAN HISTORY OF NAMING

Description

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Apart from the days of the week, Akan names are also given according to the gender of the person.

Here is a list of the names:

Male
  • Sunday.......Kwasi
  • Monday......Kwadwo
  • Tuesday....Kwabena
  • Wednesday....Kwaku
  • Thursday.......Yaw
  • Friday........Kofi
  • Saturday.....Kwame
Female
  • Sunday.....Akosua
  • Monday......Adwoa
  • Tuesday....Abenaa
  • Wednesday....Akua
  • Thursday......Yaa
  • Friday.......Afua
  • Saturday......Ama
